Common questions

We've got answers

How accurate is the 90-day forecast?
Across our 12,000+ business customer base, our forecast is 91% accurate at 30 days and 83% accurate at 90 days. We use historical seasonality, committed receivables, and recurring expense patterns to model your cash position.
Does connecting my bank affect my credit score?
No. Connecting your bank via Open Banking or Plaid is a read-only connection. We never initiate transactions, and the connection does not appear on your credit report.
How quickly can I get credit access?
Most customers see pre-qualified credit options within 24 hours of connecting. Approvals for revenue-based advances typically take 24–48 hours. Invoice financing can fund within the same business day.
Can my accountant access Headroom?
Yes. Every plan includes accountant access. You can invite your CA or bookkeeper with read-only or edit permissions, share scenarios, and export reports directly from the platform.
